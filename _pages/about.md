      <h2>About Me</h2>
      <p>
        I am a postdoctoral researcher at Southwest Jiaotong University. My
        research focuses on <span class="text-hl">AI for wireless networks</span>,
        <span class="text-hl">next-generation Wi-Fi optimization</span>,
        <span class="text-hl">multi-agent reinforcement learning</span>,
        <span class="text-hl">federated learning</span>, and
        <span class="text-hl">generative AI for resource allocation</span>.
      </p>
      <p>
        My work studies how learning-based methods can make future wireless
        systems more adaptive, efficient, and intent-aware. I am broadly
        interested in robust AI-driven optimization for networked systems and
        welcome academic collaboration.
      </p>
    </section>
